To multiply two numbers, consider the problem as a repeated addition.

Therefore $a\times b$ can be thought of as adding $a$ to itself $b$ times.

\begin{align*}
a \times b &= a + ... + a \; (b\text{ times}) \\
&= 3+3 \\
&= 6
\end{align*}
